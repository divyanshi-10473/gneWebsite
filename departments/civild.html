About Us

The Civil Engineering Department was established in 1956 and functions as a Regional Research Centre of IKG Punjab Technical University (PTU). It houses a QIP Centre approved by AICTE for pursuing Ph.D. research from 2011 to 2024. The department offers a UG program (B.Tech. in Civil Engineering), which has been accredited four times by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in the years 2004, 2008, 2014, and 2022. It also runs PG programs, including M.Tech. in Structural Engineering and M.Tech. in Geotechnical Engineering, which received NBA accreditation in 2017.

The department benefits from TEQIP and FIST funding by the World Bank and DST, Government of India, respectively. It is home to the 35th chapter of the Indian Geotechnical Society, New Delhi. Additionally, it established a Centre of Excellence for Road Safety and Traffic Management in collaboration with Punjab Police in 2021. The department has also been appointed as the State Technical Agency (STA) by the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India.
